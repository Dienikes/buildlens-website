# BuildLens Suite — Complete Platform Summary

## What BuildLens Is

BuildLens is an integrated construction management platform purpose-built for construction support firms that coordinate logistics across multiple active jobsites. It consolidates purchase orders, submittals, equipment sourcing, subcontractor management, Bills of Materials, project scheduling, and client communications into a single operational hub.

BuildLens replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, email threads, shared drives, and disconnected tools that most construction support teams rely on. Every module feeds the same underlying data layer, so a change in one place (a PO approval, a schedule slip, a material substitution) automatically propagates everywhere it matters.

---

## Core Modules

### 1. Project Dashboard

The central command view for all active projects. Each project card displays real-time status across schedule, budget, submittals, and open action items. Users can drill into any project for granular detail or stay at the portfolio level for executive oversight.

Key capabilities:

- Portfolio-level view of all active, pending, and completed projects
- Project health scoring based on schedule variance, budget burn rate, and open issues
- Customizable status indicators (on track, at risk, behind, complete)
- Quick-access links to every module scoped to a specific project
- Activity feed showing recent actions across the project team
- Milestone tracking with percent-complete rollups

### 2. Purchase Order Management

End-to-end PO lifecycle from creation through receipt and reconciliation. Every PO ties back to a project, a budget line item, and a vendor record.

Key capabilities:

- PO creation with line-item detail (quantity, unit cost, spec references, delivery dates)
- Vendor selection from the approved vendor database with auto-populated contact and payment terms
- Approval routing with configurable thresholds (e.g., POs over $5,000 require PM sign-off)
- PO status tracking: Draft, Submitted, Acknowledged, Shipped, Partial Receipt, Complete, Cancelled
- Change order management tied to original PO with variance tracking
- Automatic budget impact calculations when POs are created or modified
- PDF generation of vendor-ready PO documents with company branding
- PO history and audit trail for every line item

### 3. Submittal Tracking

Manages the full submittal workflow — from initial submission through review cycles to final approval. Tracks every revision, reviewer comment, and approval status.

Key capabilities:

- Submittal log with unique numbering per project (e.g., PROJ-SUB-001)
- Document upload and version control (specs, shop drawings, product data, samples)
- Multi-party review routing (architect, engineer, owner, GC)
- Status tracking: Pending, Under Review, Approved, Approved as Noted, Revise and Resubmit, Rejected
- Automated reminders for overdue reviews
- Linked references to specification sections and drawing numbers
- Submittal register export for GC and owner reporting
- Comment threading per submittal revision

### 4. Equipment & Material Sourcing

A sourcing engine for locating, comparing, and procuring equipment and materials. Integrates with the vendor database and PO module.

Key capabilities:

- Material search by spec, model number, manufacturer, or category
- Vendor quote comparison with side-by-side pricing, lead times, and availability
- Preferred vendor flagging and historical pricing data
- Lead time tracking and alerts for long-lead items
- Substitute/alternate material recommendations with spec compliance notes
- Integration with Bills of Materials for automatic quantity pull
- Rental vs. purchase analysis for equipment decisions
- Freight and delivery cost estimation

### 5. Subcontractor Management

Central registry and performance tracking for all subcontractors. Manages prequalification, contracts, insurance, and field performance.

Key capabilities:

- Subcontractor database with trade classifications, certifications, and insurance expiration tracking
- Prequalification workflow with document collection and scoring
- Contract management with scope, value, and milestone payment tracking
- Insurance certificate tracking with automated expiration alerts
- Performance scoring based on schedule adherence, quality, safety, and responsiveness
- Subcontractor communication log (RFIs, directives, notices)
- Lien waiver tracking and collection
- Capacity and availability tracking across active projects

### 6. Bills of Materials (BOM)

Structured material takeoffs tied to project scopes. BOMs feed directly into PO creation and budget tracking.

Key capabilities:

- BOM creation by project phase, area, or system
- Line-item detail: material description, specification reference, quantity, unit, unit cost, total cost
- BOM templates for recurring project types
- Quantity adjustment with automatic cost recalculation
- BOM-to-PO conversion (select lines, generate PO with one click)
- Material status tracking: Not Ordered, On Order, In Transit, Received, Installed
- Waste factor and contingency calculations
- BOM comparison across similar projects for estimating benchmarks
- Export to Excel and PDF

### 7. Project Scheduling

Timeline management with dependency tracking, resource allocation, and milestone reporting. Designed for construction-specific scheduling needs.

Key capabilities:

- Gantt chart view with task dependencies (finish-to-start, start-to-start, etc.)
- Milestone definition and tracking
- Critical path identification and highlighting
- Resource assignment (crews, equipment, subcontractors) per task
- Baseline schedule capture with variance tracking against current schedule
- Weather delay and force majeure logging
- Two-week and three-week lookahead generation
- Schedule narrative generation for owner reporting
- Integration with submittals and POs (e.g., material delivery dates auto-populate on schedule)
- Calendar sync and notification for upcoming milestones

### 8. Client & Contact Management (CRM)

Manages all client relationships, prospect tracking, and communication history. The relationship layer that connects people to projects.

Key capabilities:

- Client database with company info, key contacts, and project history
- Prospect pipeline with stage tracking (Lead, Qualified, Proposal, Negotiation, Won, Lost)
- Communication log: emails, calls, meetings tied to client and project records
- Proposal and bid tracking with win/loss analysis
- Client satisfaction tracking and follow-up scheduling
- Contact role mapping per project (owner rep, architect, PM, superintendent)
- Automated follow-up reminders and task generation
- Document sharing portal for client-facing deliverables

### 9. Budget & Cost Tracking

Real-time financial visibility across every project. Tracks committed costs, actual costs, and forecasted costs against the original budget.

Key capabilities:

- Budget setup by CSI division, phase, or custom cost codes
- Committed cost tracking (POs, subcontracts)
- Actual cost tracking (invoices, receipts, time)
- Budget vs. actual variance reporting with trend analysis
- Cost-to-complete forecasting
- Change order impact on budget with automatic adjustments
- Cash flow projections based on billing schedule and committed costs
- Cost report generation for internal and client-facing use
- Markup and margin tracking per project and per line item

### 10. Document Management

Centralized file storage with construction-specific organization, version control, and access permissions.

Key capabilities:

- Project-based folder structure with standard construction categories (plans, specs, submittals, RFIs, contracts, photos, reports)
- Version control with check-in/check-out to prevent overwrites
- File tagging and metadata for searchability
- Access permissions by role and project
- Drawing set management with revision tracking
- Photo documentation with date, location, and tag metadata
- Transmittal generation for formal document distribution
- Bulk upload and drag-and-drop interface

### 11. Reporting & Analytics

Configurable reporting engine that pulls data from every module. Built for both field-level operators and executive stakeholders.

Key capabilities:

- Pre-built report templates: project status, budget summary, submittal log, PO register, schedule variance, subcontractor performance
- Custom report builder with drag-and-drop field selection
- Dashboard widgets with real-time KPIs (on-time delivery rate, budget health, open submittals, PO cycle time)
- Scheduled report delivery via email (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Export to PDF, Excel, and CSV
- Role-based report access (field teams see different data than executives)
- Trend analysis across project portfolio

### 12. Notifications & Workflow Automation

Event-driven alerts and automated workflows that keep the team informed and reduce manual follow-up.

Key capabilities:

- Configurable notification rules (e.g., notify PM when PO exceeds $10K, alert team when submittal is overdue by 3 days)
- Email and in-app notifications
- Automated status transitions (e.g., PO moves to "Complete" when all items received)
- Task generation from events (e.g., new submittal creates review task for architect)
- Escalation rules for overdue items
- Daily digest emails summarizing action items per user
- Webhook support for integration with external tools

## User Roles

| Role | Access Level | Typical User |
|---|---|---|
| Admin | Full platform access, user management, system configuration | Company owner, operations manager |
| Project Manager | Full project-level access across all modules | PM, project engineer |
| Field User | Read/write on assigned projects, limited to relevant modules | Superintendent, foreman, field coordinator |
| Accounting | Budget, cost tracking, PO approval, invoice management | Controller, bookkeeper |
| Client (External) | Read-only portal access to project status, documents, schedule | Owner, owner's rep, architect |
| Subcontractor (External) | Limited portal access to their scope, documents, schedule | Sub PMs, sub foremen |

## Integration Points

BuildLens is designed to connect with the tools construction teams already use:

- **Accounting Software**: QuickBooks, Sage, Foundation — sync POs, invoices, and cost data
- **Scheduling Tools**: Microsoft Project, Primavera P6 — import/export schedule data
- **Cloud Storage**: Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, Box — file sync and backup
- **Communication**: Microsoft Teams, Slack, email — notification delivery and conversation logging
- **Estimating**: Bluebeam, PlanSwift — import takeoff data into BOMs
- **ERP Systems**: SAP, Procore, Autodesk Construction Cloud — bidirectional data exchange
- **CAD/BIM**: Autodesk Revit, AutoCAD — drawing and model reference linking

## Technical Architecture Summary

- **Frontend**: Responsive web application accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers
- **Backend**: RESTful API architecture with role-based access control
- **Database**: Relational database with full audit logging on all records
- **Hosting**: Cloud-hosted with 99.9% uptime SLA
- **Security**: SOC 2 compliant, encrypted at rest and in transit, SSO support, MFA enforcement
- **Offline**: Progressive web app capabilities for field use with limited connectivity — syncs when connection is restored

## What Makes BuildLens Different

BuildLens is not a generic project management tool adapted for construction. It is built from the ground up by people who have coordinated construction logistics in the field. Every module reflects how construction support teams actually work:

- POs reference spec sections and delivery windows, not abstract line items
- Submittals follow the actual review-and-return cycle with architect, engineer, and owner loops
- Scheduling accounts for weather, long-lead materials, and inspection holds
- Subcontractor tracking includes insurance, lien waivers, and prequalification — not just contact info
- BOMs convert directly to POs without rekeying data
- Budget tracking uses CSI divisions and construction cost codes, not generic categories

The platform eliminates double-entry, reduces communication gaps between office and field, and gives every stakeholder — from the project manager to the client — a single source of truth for project data.
